About me

Hi,i am John, mabye you know me as J.R.R Tolkien, the writer of Lord of the rings and many other books like that. I was born in South Africa and moved to England when i was 4 years old. When i got a bit older i joined to the army and took part in WW1. i got injured during the war, and while i was hospitalized i started to be intersted in writing stories, and i wrote some by myself.

My first Story was "The Hobbit" which i wrote for my children. I was a teacher in University of Leeds, and one of my student recomended me to publish The Hobbit. I decided to do as he said, and even though i didn't except for the book to be a success, it bacame really popular, and urged me to write even more. that's how The Lord of The Rings Trilogy was born, and not only this trilogy, but a whole new world.

One of my biggest interest is linguistics. i found languages really interesting, and i even created some new languages. one of the reasons that i started writing was my will to give my languages a world that they could live in, and the world of the Lord of The Ring is the world for this.

I had lots of friends, and unfortunately, since the pandemic started i didn't see them, even my family can't visit me. I am writing a lot in these days to make myself busy and trying to avoid thinking of the loneliness i feel, but the more time pass, the harder it become to not think about it. That's why i created this website, to let you the opportunity to become my friend. Will you be my new friend? If the answer is yes, stay in the page, and hit the like button down in the page.
